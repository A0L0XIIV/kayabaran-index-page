My name is Baran Kaya. I was born in 1995 in Eskişehir/Turkey. I spent my secondary and high school life in Eskişehir. Then I moved to İstanbul for university. I have a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Engineering from Istanbul Technical University (ITU). I graduated from ITU in 2018 and started to work in Huawei Turkey R&D Center. After 11 Months, I leaved from my job and started to study my graduate degree in McMaster University in Hamilton/Canada. Career

After graduating from my B.Sc. degree in August 2018, I started to work in Huawei Turkey R&D Center. My role was Assistant Software Engineer and my department was Wireless DC. Our projects were mostly focused on Base/Cell Station Management and Monitor Systems. My group was working on an indoor station's software. We mostly focused on bug fixing and some requirement additions. The system was a web project that uses Java applet in browser. The serverside was written in C but we mostly worked on client which is Java and JavaScript. Later, we started to work on secondary project. it was internal website project for individual goals. We designed the system form zero and we used Angular in the frontend, Java Spring in the backend and PostgreSQL as a database. I mostly worked on database design and we started to implement the project as a team. But then other main projects blocked our secondary project and it stopped. After 11 months of Huawei journey, I leaved my job for graduate study.
